## üí° Pro Tips for App Store Success

### After Screenshots Are Captured:

1. **Consider Adding Text Overlays**
   - Use Previewed.app ($49) or Figma (free)
   - Add short captions to each screenshot
   - Highlight key features
   - Makes screenshots more informative

2. **A/B Test Your Metadata**
   - Try different promotional text every 2 weeks
   - Monitor which keywords drive downloads
   - Adjust based on App Store Connect analytics

3. **Localize for Major Markets**
   - Spanish, Chinese, Japanese
   - Increases global reach significantly
   - Can add later after initial launch

4. **Prepare for App Review**
   - Average review time: 24-48 hours
   - May ask questions about virtual currency
   - Have answers ready (it's educational, no real money)

5. **Plan Your Launch**
   - Submit on Tuesday-Thursday for faster review
   - Avoid Friday (review teams smaller on weekends)
   - Prepare social media posts
   - Email your beta testers
