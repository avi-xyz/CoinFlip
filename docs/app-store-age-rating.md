### 9. Simulated Gambling
**Answer:** **Infrequent/Mild**

**Reason:** Trading simulation resembles gambling mechanics
- Users make financial decisions (buy/sell)
- Potential for "wins" and "losses"
- Risk/reward scenarios
- Leaderboard creates competition
- **HOWEVER:** All currency is virtual, no real money involved

**Why NOT "Frequent/Intense":**
- No actual gambling
- No real money
- Educational purpose
- Can't cash out
- No addiction risk

---

### 10. Sexual Content or Nudity
**Answer:** None

**Reason:** No sexual content.

---

### 11. Contests
**Answer:** **Infrequent/Mild**

**Reason:** Leaderboard creates competitive element
- Users compete for rankings
- Based on virtual net worth
- **However:** No prizes, purely for fun/learning

---

### 12. Unrestricted Web Access
**Answer:** No

**Reason:** App doesn't include a web browser or load external web content
- Privacy/Terms links open in Safari (external)
- This doesn't count as "unrestricted web access"

---

### 13. User Generated Content
**Answer:** No

**Reason:** Users can't create or share content
- Only username and emoji selection
- No chat, comments, or posts
- No user-to-user interaction

---

## Final Age Rating: **12+**

Based on the above answers, Apple will assign: **12+**

**Reason for 12+ (not 4+):**
- Simulated gambling mechanics
- Financial trading concepts
- Competition via leaderboard
- Requires basic understanding of money/value

## Future Considerations

### If Adding These Features, Rating May Change:

**Would Keep 12+:**
- Educational tutorials
- More detailed charts
- Achievements/badges
- Price alerts

**Would Require 17+:**
- Real money integration
- User-to-user trading
- Chat/social features
- Loot boxes or randomized rewards

**Would Lower to 4+:**
- Remove leaderboard competition
- Remove "gambling-like" mechanics
- Make it purely educational (no wins/losses)
- **NOT RECOMMENDED** - Would hurt engagement
