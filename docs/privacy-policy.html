Privacy Policy for CoinFlip

Last updated: January 21, 2026

Quick Summary: CoinFlip uses minimal data to provide trading simulation features. All currency is virtual. We don't sell your data or track you across other apps.

1. Introduction

Welcome to CoinFlip ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the CoinFlip mobile application (the "App").

By using CoinFlip, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Username: You choose a username for your account
  • Avatar Emoji: You select an emoji avatar for personalization
  • Trading Data: Your virtual portfolio, holdings, and transaction history

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version (for app functionality)
  • Usage Data: App performance data, crash reports (to improve the app)
  • Anonymous Authentication: A unique identifier for your account (no email or password required)

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Real names
  • Financial information (all currency in the app is virtual)
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Photos or camera access

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide App Features: Enable trading simulation, portfolio tracking, and leaderboard functionality
  • Maintain Your Account: Store your username, avatar, and trading history
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Technical Support: Diagnose and fix technical issues

4. Third-Party Services

CoinFlip uses the following third-party services:

4.1 Supabase (Backend Infrastructure)

  • Purpose: Database hosting, user authentication, data storage
  • Data Shared: Username, avatar, trading data
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

4.2 CoinGecko API (Cryptocurrency Prices)

  • Purpose: Fetch real-time cryptocurrency prices
  • Data Shared: None (read-only API)
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.coingecko.com/en/privacy

4.3 GeckoTerminal API (Viral Coin Data)

  • Purpose: Fetch newly launched cryptocurrency data
  • Data Shared: None (read-only API)
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.geckoterminal.com/privacy

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Storage: Your data is stored securely on Supabase's cloud infrastructure with encryption
  • Security Measures: Industry-standard encryption, secure authentication, row-level security policies
  • Data Retention: Your data is retained as long as your account exists
  • No Payment Data: We don't process real money, so no payment information is collected

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access Your Data

You can view all your data within the app (Profile section shows your stats and trading history).

6.2 Delete Your Data

  • Portfolio Reset: Deletes your holdings and transaction history (keeps username/avatar)
  • Account Deletion: Sign out and reinstall the app to create a new anonymous account
  • Request Full Deletion: Email us to delete all your data from our servers

6.3 Update Your Information

You can change your username and avatar anytime in the Profile section.

7. Children's Privacy

CoinFlip is rated for users aged 12 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 12. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 12, please contact us immediately.

8. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We may share limited data only in these circumstances:

  • Leaderboard: Your username, avatar, and net worth are visible to other users on the leaderboard
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • Service Providers: With Supabase (our infrastructure provider) as necessary to provide the service

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in data centers located in different countries. By using CoinFlip, you consent to the transfer of your information to these locations.

10. Analytics and Tracking

  • No Third-Party Analytics: We don't use Google Analytics, Facebook SDK, or similar tracking tools
  • No Advertising: We don't show ads or track you for advertising purposes
  • No Cross-App Tracking: We don't track your activity across other apps or websites

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
