# CoinFlip - App Store Category Selection

**Recommendation:** **Finance** (Primary), **Education** (Secondary)

---

## Category Options Analysis

### Option 1: Finance (PRIMARY) - ⭐ RECOMMENDED
**Why Finance:**
- Core functionality is trading/portfolio management
- Users searching "crypto apps" expect Finance category
- Competitive apps are in Finance
- More downloads from Finance category
- Better ASO (App Store Optimization)
- Matches user intent

**Pros:**
✅ Accurate representation
✅ Higher discoverability
✅ Serious users find you
✅ Trusted category for money-related apps
✅ Competition validates the category

**Cons:**
❌ More competition (but that's where users look!)
❌ Users expect real trading (mitigated by clear description)

**Top Charts Competition:**
- Coinbase
- Robinhood
- Crypto.com
- Binance
- **Your advantage:** Free, educational, no-risk

---

### Option 2: Education (SECONDARY)
**Why Education:**
- App's purpose is learning
- Safe environment emphasis
- No real money = educational tool
- Appeals to schools/teachers
- Less competitive

**Pros:**
✅ Accurate for your use case
✅ Less competition
✅ Easier to rank in top charts
✅ Appeals to cautious users
✅ Parents more comfortable

**Cons:**
❌ Lower search volume
❌ Users don't think "education" when wanting crypto app
❌ May seem too basic/boring
❌ Miss users actively seeking trading tools

---

### Option 3: Games (NOT RECOMMENDED)
**Why NOT Games:**
- Not gamified enough
- Misleading to users
- App Store Review may reject
- Violates "accurate representation" guideline

**Skip this option**

---

## Recommendation: Finance + Education

### Primary Category: Finance
**Select:** Finance → Trading

**Why:**
- Most users will search in Finance
- "Crypto" is primarily Finance category
- Portfolio/trading features fit perfectly
- Higher visibility

### Secondary Category: Education
**Select:** Education → Reference

**Why:**
- Captures educational intent
- Appeals to different audience
- Shows up in both charts
- Emphasizes learning aspect
